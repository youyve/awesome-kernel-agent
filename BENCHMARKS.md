# Benchmark inventory — moved

> This inventory has moved to its own repository and been expanded from ~70 to **151 benchmarks**:
>
> ## → [github.com/youyve/awesome-kernel-benchmark](https://github.com/youyve/awesome-kernel-benchmark)

The dedicated catalog adds a data-driven, faceted organization that this single file could not carry:

- **Two layers** — purpose-built agent benchmarks vs foundational substrate suites — across **13 families**.
- A multi-tag schema (abstraction level × Berkeley computational motif × bottleneck × hardware × precision × verify × ships-kernels × used-by-agents), defined in [`SCHEMA.md`](https://github.com/youyve/awesome-kernel-benchmark/blob/main/SCHEMA.md).
- A [motif × family coverage matrix](https://github.com/youyve/awesome-kernel-benchmark#motif--family-coverage-matrix) and the [agent ↔ substrate map](https://github.com/youyve/awesome-kernel-benchmark#agent--substrate-map).
- A machine-readable single source of truth, [`data/benchmarks.yaml`](https://github.com/youyve/awesome-kernel-benchmark/blob/main/data/benchmarks.yaml) → generated tables.

The four comparability anchors remain **PolyBench · NPB · XSBench · HeCBench**.
